How to Play "Cards Against Humanity"
As a Judge:
1. Click on the button to generate a topic card
2. Decide who the which player picked the best card for that topic
3. Add player to scoreboard or add a point to their score
4. Pick another card
5. End the game when players are out of cards
As a Player:
1. Click on the button to generate a hand of cards
2. Decide which of your cards best fits the topic card shown by the judge
3. Click on your chosen card and show the judge
4. Press the x icon and play another card for the next round
5. End the game when you are out of cards
